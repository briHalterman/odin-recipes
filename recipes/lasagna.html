Lasagna

Description

A hearty and comforting Italian-American favorite, this lasagna layers tender sheets of pasta with a rich, meaty tomato sauce, creamy ricotta filling, and plenty of gooey melted mozzarella. Each bite offers the perfect balance of savory beef, fragrant herbs, and velvety cheese. This dish is baked until bubbling and golden, making it an irresistible centerpiece for family dinners or cozy gatherings. Best served with garlic bread and a simple salad.

Ingredients

  • Mozarella
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Italian Sausage
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Brown Italian sausage in skillet.
  2. Layer pan with sauce, noodles, meat and mozarella. Repeat.
  3. Bake at 350Â° until hot all the way through and cheese is golden brown.
