Curry

Description

Curry is a flavorful and aromatic dish with countless regional variations, typically made by simmering meat, vegetables, or legumes in a richly spiced sauce. Originating from South Asia and adapted across the globe, curry can be mild or fiery, creamy or tomato-based, and is often seasoned with a blend of spices like turmeric, cumin, coriander, and chili. Whether itâ€™s a Thai green curry with coconut milk, an Indian butter chicken, or a Jamaican curry goat, the dish is known for its bold, warming flavors and is usually served with rice or flatbread to soak up the delicious sauce.

Ingredients

  • Vegetables
  • Coconut Milk
  • Curry Paste
  • Jasmine Rice

Steps

  1. Heat aeromatics with curry paste in large sauce pan.
  2. Add remaining vegetables and heat until cooked.
  3. Add coconut milk and let simmer.
  4. Serve over Jasmine rice.
